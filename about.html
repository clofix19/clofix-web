MEET THE FOUNDER

Md Shariful Islam

Founder & Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)

Md Shariful Islam is the visionary founder behind CloFix InfoTech, a next-generation cloud security and DevSecOps company. With over 15+ years of experience in DevOps, system administration, and cybersecurity, he established CloFix to bridge the gap between enterprise-grade security and accessible automation.

Shariful is a certified cybersecurity and cloud professional, holding credentials as a Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH), Certified SOC Analyst, Certified Hacking Forensic Investigator, AWS Certified Solutions Architect, Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA), and AWS Certified DevOps Engineer (DOP).

His expertise spans CloFix WAF development, multi-cloud security (AWS, Azure, OpenStack), Kubernetes orchestration, infrastructure automation (Terraform, Ansible), and CI/CD pipeline optimization. He has successfully led DevOps teams, managed infrastructure for 30+ enterprise clients, and implemented automation solutions that reduced operational costs by up to 40% while improving scalability and security.
