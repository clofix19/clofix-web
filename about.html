About CloFix

CloFix is a next-generation cloud security and DevOps solutions provider specializing in enterprise-grade Web Application Firewall (WAF), intelligent cloud automation, expert DevOps support, and on-demand engineering assistance. We empower businesses of all sizes to enhance security, streamline operations, and scale efficiently across multi-cloud and hybrid environments. Powered by AI and industry best practices, our team delivers proactive, scalable solutions built on deep expertise in cloud platforms and security frameworks.

Driven by a mission to make advanced cybersecurity and automation accessible to all, CloFix leads with intelligent, preventive protection. Our AI-driven approach anticipates threats before they occur, ensuring infrastructures remain secure, optimized, and self-managed, allowing businesses to innovate confidently without compromise.
