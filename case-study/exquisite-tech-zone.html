📍 About Exquisite Tech Zone

Exquisite Tech Zone (etechz.com) is a fast-growing software and security solutions company based in Bangladesh. Specializing in innovative digital products, custom development, and secure IT systems, they empower businesses with scalable, modern technology - from enterprise software to cybersecurity infrastructure.

As a authorized Solution Partner of CloFix, Exquisite Tech Zone not only resells and deploys CloFix WAF for clients but also relies on the same advanced Web Application Firewall to secure its own digital assets, internal tools, and client-facing portals.

⚡ Key pain points: DDoS attacks & unprotected surface

Before partnering with CloFix, Exquisite Tech Zone faced escalating cybersecurity challenges that threatened business continuity:

  • Repeated DDoS attacks - Frequent layer 7 HTTP floods and volumetric attacks causing service degradation and downtime during peak business hours.
  • SQL injection & XSS attempts - Daily exploit probes targeting customer portals and admin panels.
  • Legacy firewall limitations - No real-time visibility, manual rule updates, and zero bot management capabilities.
  • Slow incident response - Alert fatigue and delayed mitigation without a dedicated support SLA.
  • Partner demo environment risk - Needed enterprise-grade security without complexity or upfront cost.
🔍 Impact of DDoS attacks:
• Website unavailable for hours at a time → lost revenue & client trust.
• Origin server overloaded → degraded performance for legitimate users.
• Manual mitigation efforts drained engineering resources.
