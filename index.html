Our Services

CloFix delivers powerful security, automation, and infrastructure solutions tailored for cloud-native and enterprise-grade applications. We specialize in Web Application Firewall (WAF), DevSecOps, and intelligent cloud automation enabling businesses to operate securely, efficiently, and at scale. With real-time threat detection, dynamic policy enforcement, and full-stack observability, CloFix empowers organizations to stay resilient against evolving cyber threats. Whether you're running SaaS platforms, e-commerce portals, or mission-critical APIs, CloFix ensures performance, protection, and peace of mind 24/7.

CloFix - WAF

CloFix WAF is a fully managed Web Application Firewall designed to protect modern web applications, APIs, and digital services against advanced threats, bot attacks, CDN Service, Wordpress Protection and OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities all with minimal latency and maximum scalability..

CloFix - SRE Support

CloFix SRE Support ensures fast, secure, and scalable application delivery by embracing modern Site Reliability Engineering principles. Our team automates deployment pipelines, optimizes infrastructure, and implements real-time observability to maintain high availability, performance, and reliability for cloud-native applications.

CloFix - Automation & Management

CloFix provides reliable, secure, and cost-effective cloud automation and infrastructure management services. We help businesses streamline operations, reduce manual effort, and achieve continuous availability.

CloFix - On-Demand Engineering Support

CloFix On-Demand Engineering Support delivers fast, expert-level technical assistance exactly when you need it. Our seasoned engineers help you troubleshoot, optimize, and secure your infrastructure, deployments, and DevOps pipelines ensuring resilience, efficiency, and reliability at every step.
