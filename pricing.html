WAF Dedicated Hosting Agreement

CloFix Infotech - Standard Service Agreement (Global)

Effective: January 1, 2026 Jurisdiction: International Governing Language: English
01

Definitions & Parties

"Provider" means CloFix Infotech, a company registered under applicable law, providing WAF and cybersecurity services globally.

"Client" means the legal entity or individual that executes this Agreement or clicks "Accept" on the online order form.

"Service" means the CloFix WAF Dedicated Hosting package selected by the Client, as described in the applicable Order Form.

"Effective Date" means the date the Client's order is confirmed in writing or electronically by the Provider.

"Subscription Term" means the initial contract period (12 months unless otherwise stated) and any renewal periods.

02

Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver the following under the selected Dedicated Hosting tier:

  • Dedicated WAF reverse-proxy infrastructure (isolated VM or bare-metal, as specified)
  • CloFix WAF software license for the contracted number of domains
  • Full OWASP Core Rule Set (CRS) engine with AI-powered behavioral analysis
  • DDoS mitigation at L3/L4/L7 layers with volumetric, protocol, and application-layer detection
  • WASM module execution engine (TinyGo / Rust / C / AssemblyScript)
  • Lua & JavaScript custom scripting engines
  • JA3/JA4 TLS fingerprinting and device behavioral analysis
  • ClickHouse-backed analytics, logging, and real-time dashboards
  • Monthly maintenance: security patches, rule updates, version upgrades
  • Initial onboarding, DNS migration support, and SSL provisioning
03

Deployment & Infrastructure

The Client may elect one of the following deployment models, subject to availability and tier eligibility:

Client's Cloud AWS, GCP, Azure, DigitalOcean, Linode, Vultr, or any VPS provider. Client retains cloud billing. Provider manages the WAF layer.
Provider Datacenter Hosted on CloFix-managed servers. Infrastructure costs included in the annual hosting fee.
On-Premise Client-owned hardware. Provider delivers remote installation, configuration, and ongoing management. Enterprise tier only.
Hybrid Primary on Provider datacenter, failover on Client cloud. Available on Business & Enterprise tiers.

05

Payment Terms & Billing

  • Annual Hosting & License: Invoiced annually in advance on the Effective Date anniversary.
  • Monthly Maintenance: Invoiced on the 1st of each calendar month.
  • Setup Fee: Invoiced on the Effective Date; due before provisioning begins.
  • Payment Window: Net-7 days from invoice date.
  • Late Payment: 1.5% per month (18% per annum) on overdue balances.
  • Currencies Accepted: BDT (Bangladeshi Taka), USD, EUR, GBP, SGD.
  • Payment Methods: Bank transfer (SWIFT / local), bKash, Nagad, Payoneer, Wise, Stripe.
  • Taxes: All fees are exclusive of applicable taxes (VAT, GST, withholding tax). Client is responsible for taxes in their jurisdiction.
  • Price Adjustments: Provider may adjust pricing with 60 days' written notice prior to renewal. No mid-term price increases.
06

Term, Renewal & Termination

  • Initial Term: 12 months from the Effective Date, unless a custom term is specified in the Order Form.
  • Auto-Renewal: Automatically renews for successive 12-month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 30 days before the term end.
  • Early Termination by Client: Client may terminate with 30 days' written notice. No refund of pre-paid annual fees; monthly maintenance ceases at termination date.
  • Termination for Cause: Either party may terminate this Agreement under the following conditions: CloFix. reserves the right to terminate the Service immediately at its sole discretion, including for policy violations, misuse, non-payment, or security risks. The Client may terminate the Agreement by providing a minimum of thirty (30) days prior written notice, provided that all outstanding payments and obligations have been fully cleared before termination becomes effective..
  • Data Handling: Upon termination, all Client data will be permanently destroyed at the end of the notice period.
