Common Healthcare Cyber Threats

CloFix WAF protects healthcare organizations from these critical attacks

🔓

Data Breaches

Attackers steal sensitive patient data including medical records, personal information, and payment data.

🔒

Ransomware

Ransomware attacks encrypt healthcare systems and demand payment to restore access to critical patient data.

📊

Medical Identity Theft

Attackers steal patient identities to commit fraud, obtain medical services, or file false insurance claims.

⚡

DDoS Attacks

Volumetric attacks that disrupt patient portals, hospital websites, and critical healthcare services.

📝

Phishing Attacks

Attackers target healthcare staff with phishing emails to gain access to sensitive systems and data.

🔌

API Abuse

Attackers exploit healthcare APIs to access patient data, medical records, and scheduling systems.
